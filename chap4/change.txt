Established on September 16, 1991, Macao Polytechnic Institute (IPM) is a public
institution of higher education with an emphasis on applied knowledge and
skills. IPM’s motto is “knowledge, expertise and global vision”, and the
Institute strives to achieve international standards in teaching and learning,
building an e-campus, ensuring administrative compliance with the law and sound
research management. Small in size and compact in structure, IPM adopts the
strategy of “dual emphasis on teaching and research”, with a commitment to
remain “rooted in Macao and backed up by the motherland while facing the world
and striving for excellence”.
